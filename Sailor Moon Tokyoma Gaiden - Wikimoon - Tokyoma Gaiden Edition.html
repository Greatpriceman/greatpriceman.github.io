Sailor Moon Tokyoma Gaiden refers to the first season of the Tokyoma Gaiden anime spin off. Many fans refer to this season as "Season 0" outside Japan and North America, to differentiate it from the International first season.

Summary[edit | ]

Pinako Aino, Daughter of one of the DD Girls, Has finally been accepted into Shiba Koen Middle School with an endorsement from her aunt. While she tries to make friends in school to no avail, two girls make her life even more miserable. when these two, as well as a teacher are revealed to have been Youma sent by the Dark Agency, Pinako must take up the mantle of a Sailor Senshi, strange things are happening and it is up to three new Sailor Senshi, Sailor Pinako, Mars Warrior, and a second generation Sailor Mercury to stop the Dark Agency from influencing the Youma back into the Darkness.

Meanwhile, the boss of the Dark Agency, Ashura Kingdyes, wants to bring the Youma race to be on equal footing with humans or greater, he believes that he needs the Daughter of the Crimson General to accomplish his ends, for this plan he sends Val The Csar, National Champion of Knights & Guardians to make friends with Pinako, however after their first battle, Val finds her to be more of a rival to surpass than an asset to recruit.

Pinako and her friends must defeat the Dark Agency before something, or someone, does it for them.
